FuelGuard - Fuel Supply Chain

This dApp interacts with three separate smart contracts: FuelGuardRecord, FuelGuardAllocation, and FuelGuardVerification.

0. Grant Role (Admin only — FuelGuardRecord)

1. Record Fuel Batch (Importer — FuelGuardRecord)

2. Allocate to Wholesaler (Importer — FuelGuardAllocation)

3. Distribute to Retailer (Wholesaler — FuelGuardAllocation)

4. Confirm Delivery (Retailer — FuelGuardAllocation)

5. View Batch Details (FuelGuardVerification — read-only)

6. Check Stakeholder Holdings (FuelGuardVerification — anti-hoarding)
